The rise of streaming services has significantly altered the way people consume movies. This shift has been driven by the increasing popularity of platforms like Netflix and Hulu. As a result, the traditional movie-watching experience has undergone a substantial transformation. The ease of access to a vast library of content has changed viewer habits. Streaming has made it possible for people to watch movies at their convenience. 
   <Callout type="takeaway"> Streaming has revolutionized the way we watch movies.</Callout>
   ## What happened
   The advent of streaming services has led to a decline in physical movie sales and rentals. Many consumers now prefer the convenience of streaming over traditional methods. This change has forced the entertainment industry to adapt to new distribution models. 
   The impact of streaming on the movie industry has been profound. It has enabled new revenue streams and changed the way movies are marketed. 
   Streaming services have also given rise to new types of content, such as original series and movies.
   ## Why it matters
   The shift to streaming has significant implications for the entertainment industry. It has changed the way movies are produced, distributed, and consumed. The rise of streaming has also led to new opportunities for content creators. 
   The stakes are high for traditional movie studios, as they must adapt to the changing landscape. 
   The impact of streaming will be felt for years to come, as it continues to shape the entertainment industry.

   ## How to think about it
   To navigate this new landscape, consumers should consider the benefits and drawbacks of streaming. They should think about the types of content they want to access and the platforms that best provide it. 
   The entertainment industry must also be forward-thinking, embracing new technologies and distribution models. 
   By understanding the implications of streaming, both consumers and the industry can make informed decisions about how to move forward.
