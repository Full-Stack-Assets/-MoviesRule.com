The highly anticipated wedding of pop superstar Taylor Swift and NFL star Travis Kelce took place over the holiday weekend at Madison Square Garden in New York City. The star-studded event, which drew approximately 1,000 guests to the iconic arena, has quickly captured the attention of fans and media outlets worldwide. Now, the groom's mother, Donna Kelce, has offered her first public reaction to the high-profile nuptials. Her brief but heartfelt comments provide a rare glimpse into what is already being called one of the biggest celebrity weddings of the decade.

## What happened

The wedding festivities kicked off on Thursday night with a 100-person rehearsal dinner held at the Infosys Theater inside Madison Square Garden. The main ceremony took place the following day on Friday, with the marriage officially confirmed to the public when a "JusT&T Married!" sign appeared on the screens outside the arena. Both Swift and Kelce wore custom haute couture designs by Jonathan Anderson for Christian Dior.

While the guest list was massive, the event maintained an intimate atmosphere. Actor Adam Sandler officiated the ceremony, while the couple's respective brothers, Austin Swift and Jason Kelce, served as the man of honor and best man. Entertainment for the evening included performances by Swift alongside legendary musicians Stevie Nicks and Paul McCartney.

During an interview posted on Macy's official Instagram account on Sunday, Donna Kelce was asked to share her thoughts on the nuptials. Although she kept specific details private, she described the experience as "magical, man, magical." She also shared a nostalgic holiday memory of taking her sons to watch Fourth of July fireworks in Euclid, Ohio.
