Indie horror sensation Obsession has finally left the theater and landed on streaming services, giving fans a chance to watch the film from their couch. In addition to Peacock, the title is available for rent or purchase on Amazon Prime Video and Apple TV. Its rapid move to streaming reflects the film’s strong box‑office performance and growing cult following.

## What happened
Obsession debuted in theaters earlier this summer and quickly became a box‑office hit, prompting a swift transition to digital platforms.

On July 17 the film was added to Peacock’s catalog; an ad‑supported subscription costs $10.99 per month, while the ad‑free Premium Plus tier is $17 per month and includes offline downloads.

Viewers who prefer not to subscribe can still rent or buy the movie on Amazon Prime Video or Apple TV, where it is offered at the standard digital rental price.

For viewers, the availability means they can experience the film’s unsettling twist without waiting for a physical release.
