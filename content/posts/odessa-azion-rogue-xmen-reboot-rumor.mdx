Rumors are swirling again around Marvel’s upcoming X‑Men reboot, this time focusing on the iconic mutant Rogue. While Marvel Studios has stayed silent, director Jake Schreier’s recent comments have kept the conversation alive. The speculation matters because Rogue’s casting could shape the tone and diversity of the new franchise. Fans and streaming watchers are watching closely for any official word.

## What happened
Recent reports from Yahoo! News cite Jeff Sneider’s months‑old scoop that Odessa A'zion was selected to play Rogue in the X‑Men reboot. The claim resurfaced after other outlets repeated the story, noting A'zion’s recent credits in Stranger Things: Tales from ’85, I Love LA, and other projects.

Marvel Studios has not issued a statement confirming or denying the rumor. Director Jake Schreier acknowledged the ongoing discussion but offered no details about the final casting decisions. The lack of official confirmation leaves the rumor in a gray area, but it continues to dominate online conversations.
