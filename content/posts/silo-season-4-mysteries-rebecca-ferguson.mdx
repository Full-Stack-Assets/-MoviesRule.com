This unique filming schedule means the definitive end of the dystopian series is already locked in, promising fans a structured conclusion to the underground mystery.

## What happened

In a recent interview with Collider, star Rebecca Ferguson and showrunner Graham Yost discussed the production status and narrative trajectory of the Apple TV+ sci-fi series. With Season 3 set to premiere, the creators revealed that Season 4 has already wrapped filming. Ferguson admitted that shooting the final two seasons close together led to some confusion over which events occurred in which season, though she noted the final days on set were highly emotional for the cast and crew.

According to Yost and Ferguson, Season 3 will feature numerous 'aha' moments that challenge viewers' expectations and shift the direction of the narrative. While Season 3 focuses on Juliette Nichols dealing with lost memories and the aftermath of the rebellion, Season 4 is designed to deliver concrete answers to the questions raised. Yost emphasized that the final season will focus on survival and how the ultimate fates of the characters play out.
