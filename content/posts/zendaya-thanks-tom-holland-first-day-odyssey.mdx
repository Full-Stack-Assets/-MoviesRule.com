## What happened

Zendaya arrived at the New York City premiere dressed as a winged goddess, a look inspired by the owl of Athena that she described as a literal interpretation of the goddess of wisdom. In the interview she explained that the costume was part of a long‑planned vision by the costume designer, allowing her to embody the mythic qualities of her role.

She also recounted that Tom Holland was present on her very first day of filming, offering a calming presence as she stepped onto the expansive Christopher Nolan set. She highlighted the scale and scope of the production as “extraordinary,” noting that having her husband there helped mitigate the daunting nature of the shoot.

Tom Holland, when asked, expressed enthusiasm for working alongside Zendaya, calling the experience “awesome” and praising her performance under the demanding conditions of a Nolan epic.
