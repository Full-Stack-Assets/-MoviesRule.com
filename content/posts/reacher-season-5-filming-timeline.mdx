The popular Prime Video series Reacher is set to begin filming its fifth season soon. Alan Ritchson, who stars as the titular character, confirmed that filming will start on Monday. The scripts for the new season are already done, and Ritchson will be busy with the show for the rest of the year.

## What happened
Reacher season 4 is gearing up to premiere in August 2026, adapting the thirteenth novel in the book franchise, 2009's Gone Tomorrow. Despite the upcoming release, Ritchson revealed that the team is ready to move on to season 5. The filming update comes after Prime Video unveiled their release plans for both Reacher season 4 and the upcoming spinoff series Neagley.

The confirmation that season 5 is set to begin filming before the end of July suggests that the next season may have been developed or partially written around the time of Reacher season 4. This would not be unusual, as prior seasons have had varying turnaround times between official and secret greenlights.
