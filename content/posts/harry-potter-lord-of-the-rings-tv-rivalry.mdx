This rivalry initially began in the early 2000s when both franchises were brought to life on the big screen. The trend of reboots and revivals has increased in recent years, and the fantasy genre hasn't been exempt from it. With the rise of TV shows, franchises like Percy Jackson have gotten a second chance, and now Harry Potter is joining them with an HBO TV show.

## What happened
The early 2000s were significant years for the fantasy genre, with the release of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone in November 2001, followed by The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring in December 2001. Both films were critically and commercially successful, allowing them to continue adapting the rest of the books in their respective series. The Harry Potter movie saga ended in 2011, while The Lord of the Rings trilogy ended in 2003.

The Lord of the Rings currently has a live-action TV series, The Rings of Power, streaming on Prime Video. The show is based on the appendices of the first novel in the Lord of the Rings series and is set thousands of years before the events of the book. The Rings of Power premiered in 2022 and is preparing for its third season, set for a November 2026 release. Season 1 of HBO's Harry Potter TV show will be released on December 25, 2026.

The winter of 2026 will see Harry Potter and The Lord of the Rings going head-to-head one more time, this time on TV, with overlapping release schedules. The Rings of Power season 3 will have a weekly release format, as will the Harry Potter TV show.
